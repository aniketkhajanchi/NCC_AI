Verify/update SM configuration

The SM local data was recovered from Backup and Recovery, so the changes performed to the local SM configuration between the time the backup was taken and the time the disaster occurred (if any) have not been recovered. You must to login to the SM and verify that the application preferences and other configuration parameters are up to date. It may be necessary to re-load the details of the Managed Elements.
