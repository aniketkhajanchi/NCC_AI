Distribution rules for aggregated files

Distribution rules define where specific output records are sent. One record can be sent to multiple outputs if needed. The destination of the output record depends on the type of output it has configured. Records can be sent to files, where they are divided into different file groups, or they can be sent to a Kafka topic.

Aggregation output files consist of final and partial files. Final and partial files are distributed to different destinations (directory/Kafka topic).
