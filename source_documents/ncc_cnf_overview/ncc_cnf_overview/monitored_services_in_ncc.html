Monitored services in NCC

Services vital to the SM subsystem (part of an IG system)

  • SM app (API server)
  • User and role management (made vital in NCC Release 22.3)

Services vital to the ME subsystem

  • Provisioning (made vital in NCC Release 22.5)
  • Diameter load balancer (IOHD)
  • Charging

Services vital to both SM and ME subsystems

  • Aerospike DB cluster
  • Kafka (brokers)
  • Lock and Transaction Manager
  • Tomb Raider Scheduler (TRS) (on CM pods)
  • Rollback Manager (on CM pods)
  • Timed Event Manager (TEM) (on CM pods)
  • XDR (DB replication from other NCCs)

Other non-vital monitored services

These services do not force the subsystem out of service, if the service is down.
  • Recharge
  • Notification
  • TMF
