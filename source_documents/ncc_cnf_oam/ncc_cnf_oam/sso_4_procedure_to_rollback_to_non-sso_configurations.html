Procedure to rollback to non-SSO/LDAP configurations

Review the prerequisites before you proceed with the rollbacks.

Note:
  • In deployments with integrated (IG) systems, the ME subsystem must be modified in the same maintenance window as the SM subsystem. In deployments that have IG with additional ME systems, one or more of the additional ME systems must be modified in the same maintenance window as the IG.
  • CGF (DR) chart (Helm releasename=dr) must be rolled back.
  • Metrics Viewer must be rolled back.

The rollback procedure is for four SM/IG systems (1, 2, 3 and 4) as the use case. In the case of only two SM/IG systems, certain steps can be omitted.

Verify the MariaDB replication status on all SM/IG systems and ensure all pods are in sync and replicating with each other. It can take a few minutes for the replication status to display updated results. If the verification commands to check replication status indicate problems, see SSO/LDAP migration troubleshooting.
