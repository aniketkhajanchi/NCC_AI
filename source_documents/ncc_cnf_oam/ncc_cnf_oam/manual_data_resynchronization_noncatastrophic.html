Manual data resynchronization (non-catastrophic)

On occasion, Aerospike data sync may fall behind. If the data cannot be auto-synced, the data may need to be resynced manually

This is a non-catastrophic disaster where data is not getting synced between a mated pair. The Kubernetes objects are fine, it is only the data that is the problem. One of the mated pairs has fallen behind in syncing data from another. This could be because of slowness or any other WAN issue. The recovery procedure should be carried out after resolving the root cause of the disaster.
