Log level configuration

Log levels are configurable in NCC. Log levels may be configured for specific classes on individual pods or across pod types, and configuration changes do not require a reboot. Configurations are preserved through pod restarts. Log level changes can be applied to startup and initialization classes, but a default log4j may be used without installation time changes.
