Deleting product

The following procedure describes how to delete a product in the CCTF UI.

In the product list, click the three-dot button besides the name of the test package to be deleted, and select Delete, for example:

Depending on whether the product has test packages under it:

  1. The product is deleted, if it has no test packages under it.
  2. If the product has test packages under it, a confirmation pop-up window opens. Click DELETE to remove the product.
    Note:
    Deleting a product deletes permanently also all test packages under it.
