Data redundancy across subsystems

Data is always replicated from one NCC subsystem to another for redundancy. With large numbers of subsystem instances, it is not possible to replicate data to all subsystems, so subsystems are paired up.

Within a pair of subsystem instances, data is exchanged and device records are identified as being owned by one subsystem or another. Only the subsystem that owns a device can write its data. This is to ensure data integrity and avoid two sites writing the same record.
