Notification for call rejection due to hard-stop

Notification can be sent to a subscriber when the call cannot be established due to the entityCounter usage limit value with Reject action. Following are the possible scenarios:
  • New call cannot be established
  • Ongoing call cannot reserve new quota
Such notifications can be sent based on the configurations made, such as:
  • Configured times per period (configured once, times, or always per period) for each entityCounter: This can be applicable for all call types such as IMS/SMS/MMS/PS.
  • Configured daily for each entityCounter: This can be applicable for one notification per day for PS call.
  • Existing NCC behavior.
