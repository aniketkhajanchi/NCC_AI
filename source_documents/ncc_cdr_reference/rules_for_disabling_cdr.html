Rules for disabling CDR

By default, Charging CDR is always enabled. To disable it, the following actions must be configured in rules:

  • To disable Charging CDR, use the action GY_PRE_PROCESSING.Disable-Charging-CDR (available in the trigger: GY_PRE_PROCESSING).

  • To disable Charging notification CDR, use the action NOTIFICATION.Disable-Charging-Notification-CDR (available in the trigger: RATING_POST_PROCESSING).
