Known limits in NCC

This section provides information about known limits (that is, minimum and maximum values) for some parameters. These limits are based on observations from multiple factors like lab testings/customer-specific environment. It is recommended to verify these limits in customer specific environment.

Max subscriptions

  • Create-all profile in /services/ServiceManager/subscriber/create/Profile API supports max 30 subscriptions
  • Delete-all profile in /services/ServiceManager/subscriber/delete/Profile API supports max 30 subscriptions

Above values are based on the following lab testing environment:

  • Lab with (CPU-272, RAM-828 GB)
  • 30 subscriptions per device
  • 4 Custom Data per device
  • 1 Charging Logic per Service
  • 1 Bucket per Charging Logic
Note:
The performance may vary with complexity of static data.
