Aborting executing job

The following procedure describes how to abort a job that is being executed.

  1. In the Execution Results view, click the Abort job button in the end of the row of the running job you want to abort, for example:

    A confirmation pop-up is displayed.

  2. Click ABORT to abort the execution of the job. The job is forced to stop.
    Note:
    Aborting an executing job might cause unexpected results to the system under test.
