Scaling

Scaling options

Scaling means increasing or decreasing the number of pods based on the resources that are needed. Scale-in decreases the number of running pods of a particular pod type if the traffic is sufficiently low to recognize that fewer resources are required. Scale-out increases the number of pods of a particular pod type to provide additional capacity. Scale-in and scale-out can be done manually or using autoscaling operation in Kubernetes.
