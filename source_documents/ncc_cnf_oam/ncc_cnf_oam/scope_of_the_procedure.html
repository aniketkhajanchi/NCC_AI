Scope of the procedure

Note:
This procedure does not recover SM/IG. For SM/IG recovery steps, see Disaster recovery for SM systems.
Note:
This procedure is only for use with ME deployments that utilize partition schemes such as 1+1 with 2 partitions. This procedure does not work with deployments that utilize a partition scheme of 1+1 with 1 partition.

This procedure covers recovery after the failure of a system or subsystem, either network or independent pair. Specifically, these deployments might utilize this procedure for disaster recovery:

  • Any one CHF in a 1+1 ME Pair deployment
