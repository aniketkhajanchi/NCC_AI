Charging notification EDRs

Charging notification EDRs are generated as a result of notification events in the Charging application. The deviceIdtag indicates that the notification is for device and the groupID tag indicates that the notification is for group.

The serviceProvider indicates the service provider (MNO/MVNO) for which the CDR is applicable. This tag is generated only if service provider is enabled.
