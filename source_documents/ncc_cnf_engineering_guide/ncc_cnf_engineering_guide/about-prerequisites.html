Prerequisites

It is assumed that the reader is familiar with the NCC architecture and feature set. It also assumes that the reader is familiar with containerization

To perform the tasks in this documentation set, you need sufficient technical knowledge about:

  • 3GPP specifications, available at: http://www.3gpp.org/

  • general telecommunications principles

  • network requirements, recommended deployments, and administration

  • UNIX administration
