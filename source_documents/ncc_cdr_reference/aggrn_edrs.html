EDR aggregation

EDRs with recordType as Provisioning_Record or RechargeRecord sharing the same correlationID can be aggregated to create a single output.

EDRs that share the same correlationID are ordered by aggSequenceNumber tag. The aggregation process starts once CGF receives an EDR with aggSequenceNumber = 1 till receiving an EDR with aggLastRecord = true.
