Mated subsystems and maintaining data integrity

A traditional challenge with mated subsystems is detecting split brain scenarios. In this scenario, the two subsystems cannot communicate with each other and are not sure which one should become active for given subscribers. There is no way to know whether the mate is actually failed, or whether there is simply a network communication issue between the two sites. To address this challenge, NCC employs a network heartbeat strategy where all other subsystems in the network communicate their view of the health of all other subsystems. This enables a given NCC to have a more diverse view of network status and to differentiate between a mate failure case verses simply a loss of communication from the local NCC subsystem to the mate NCC subsystem.
