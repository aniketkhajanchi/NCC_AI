Web SSO data recovery

Once the installation is complete, perform the steps described in Web SSO data recovery for non-catastrophic disaster, to retrieve a User and Role Management MariaDB backup from a healthy SM and restore it on SM4. Skip the steps in Verify MariaDB replication, as the replication is disabled at this point. You can verify the replication after all of the data is restored and a Helm upgrade has been performed to restore network connectivity.
