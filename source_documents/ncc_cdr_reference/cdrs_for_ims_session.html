IMS CDR with multiple account information tag

If the same account is charged twice (different subscriptions) in the same transaction, then the single CDR for IMS call is generated and account related tags are present twice.

For example:

  • Overage fee is configured in a subscription counter as well as a multi-bundle counter.
  • Overage fee for subscription counter is 10 for an overage block of 100.
  • Overage fee for a multi-bundle counter is 20 for an overage block of 100.
  • Account main balance before call is 10000 and both the counters have exhausted their usage blocks completely.

In CCR(U), committed units=200, which implies 2 overage blocks of subscription counter are used whose fee is 10 each. Then 2 overage blocks of multi-bundle counter are also used whose fee is 20 each. (Since, both the counters are used in the same transaction).

Account balance Overage fee for subscription counter charged Overage fee for multi-bundle counter charged
Before 10000 9980
After 9980 9940
Committed 20 40

Next CCR(T) is received with committed units=200, which means 2 overage blocks of subscription counter are used whose fee is 10 each. Then 2 overage blocks of multi-bundle counter are also used whose fee is 20 each. (Since, both the counters are used in the same transaction).

Account balance Overage fee for subscription counter charged Overage fee for multi-bundle counter charged
Before 9940 9920
After 9920 9880
Committed 20 40

In the single CDR generated for IMS, the 2 separate domains for account tags are generated since the counters belong to different subscriptions as follows:

Table: For subscription counter
Field/tag Values
accountBalanceBefore 10000
accountBalanceAfter 9920
accountBalanceCommitted 40
Table: For multi-bundle counter
Field/tag Values
accountBalanceBefore 9980
accountBalanceAfter 9880
accountBalanceCommitted 80

Conclusion :

The accountBalanceBefore and accountBalanceAfter do not represent the actual value before and after the call but accountBalanceCommitted represents the actual values. Account balance before can be referred as the biggest of the accountBalanceBefore values and account balance after can be referred as the smallest of the accountBalanceAfter values.
