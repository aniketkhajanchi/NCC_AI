Backup and recovery

This section describes the procedures for backup and recovery of NCC. NCC Backup and Recovery is used to achieve a full backup and recovery, controlled by configured backup policies. These instructions for backup and restore assume that NCC Backup and Recovery has already been installed, and that NCC Backup and Recovery has been enabled. These instructions also assume User and Role Management, NCC Telemetry, and NCC have been installed.
