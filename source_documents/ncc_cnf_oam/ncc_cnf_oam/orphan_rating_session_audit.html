Orphan rating session audit

If a rating session exists without primary sessions such as GySession, SySession, or IMSSession, then it is called as orphan rating session.

If db-list (for session inconsistency) finds an orphan rating session, then the tool creates a TEM entry to audit such orphan rating session and sets it to expire soon. Upon audit, these events occur for the orphan rating session:
  • Reservations are cleared.
  • TEM is deleted.
  • The session is deleted.
  • Secondary index tables are not considered for update.
  • Audit CDR is dumped.
