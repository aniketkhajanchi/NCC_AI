SS7 pod-to-pod communication

SS7 pods monitor connections to downstream pods and take care routing requests to the correct pod and monitoring when pods are unavailable. The Kubernetes service concept in use throughout the rest of NCC does the equivalent functionality, but Kubernetes services are not used to route traffic in SS7.

SS7 pods do use Istio for mTLS support.

In the event of a failure, SS7 pods retry the downstream pod, but if retries are exhausted, an error is returned to the SS7 peer.
