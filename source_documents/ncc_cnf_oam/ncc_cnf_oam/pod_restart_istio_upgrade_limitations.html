Limitations

  1. During the helm upgrade of NCC Telemetry charts in all type of systems, there are some lost metrics. The helm upgrade restarts the cpro-server, a singleton pod, and during this period there are metrics losses. The time is dependent on the platform but two to three minutes is typical.
  2. During the restart of each Aerospike pod, there is a provisioning failure for 4G and 5G calls for one to two seconds.
  3. During the helm upgrade of CGF (DR) charts, there are some lost CDRs, 1% of the CDRs are lost due to the restart of the CGF (DR) pods. After this, the CDRs resume again.
  4. During the helm upgrade of SPS charts, there were 4G and 5G calls, because of IOHD failure.
  5. During the helm upgrade of SPS charts, there was loss of provisioning calls multiple times, because of Kafka.
  6. During the helm upgrade of SPS charts in NCC systems with SM subsystems, Provisioning failure, 4G and 5G failure, due to User and Role Management failures.
  7. The pod restart counter Key/Value is defined globally per site, which means it will impact all the pods in that system.
  8. Currently there is no ability to pick and choose individual pods to restart. It is either restart all or none.
