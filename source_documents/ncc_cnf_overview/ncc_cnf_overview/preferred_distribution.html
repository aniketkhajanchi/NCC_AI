Preferred distribution

In NCC, most affinity rules are used to spread pods among available worker nodes. Specifically, they use anti-affinity with preferredDuringSchedulingIgnoredDuringExecution. This means that a best attempt is made to spread out pods, but if they cannot be spread, deployment is not stopped. For example if eight worker nodes are available and 10 pods are requested, the deployment does not fail because two worker nodes have more than one pod of the same type.
