Verify MariaDB replication

After you restore the MariaDB backup on SM4, use these commands to verify the status of MariaDB replication:

 ## When using these example commands, make sure to use pod names/container names as appropriate.
kubectl exec -n $NAMESPACE -it ckey-cmdb-maxscale-0 -c maxscale -- maxscale_adm --list-servers --all

kubectl -n $NAMESPACE exec -it ckey-cmdb-mariadb-0 --container mariadb -- mariadb_adm --replication-status

Use these commands on all SMs, including SM4, to make sure the replication is in sync on all SMs.

It may take a few minutes for the replication to sync up. If problems persist even after 5 to 10 minutes, go to SM4 and scale down to 0 replicas the maxscale pods first, then scale down to 0 the MariaDB pods and then, once they are all terminated, scale them up again.
