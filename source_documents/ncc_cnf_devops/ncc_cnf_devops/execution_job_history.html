Execution job history

CCTF lists the history of all execution jobs in the Execution Results view. The following figure presents an example of the execution job history.

Figure: Example of the execution job history
Table: Execution job history data
Data Decsription
Job status Color bar in the left-hand side of each job. The colors represent the status of the jobs as follows:

  • blue - the job has executed successfully

    Note:
    Even if a job executes successfully, some of its tests might not have passed.
  • black - the job has failed
  • orange - the job is running
  • gray - the job has been aborted
  • light gray - the job is in queue
id The job identity.
Suite The test suite (button) this job belongs to.
Result The test suite execution result:
  • "blank" - the job has not finished yet
  • success (with green background) - the job has finished successfully and all tests have passed
  • fail (with red background) - the job has finished successfully but not all tests have passed, or the job itself has failed
Pass/Fail/Error/Total The number of the test suites that passed, failed, had an error, and the total number of the test cases, respectively.
Exec-time (UTC) The execution time of the job.
